Algorithm

Big Data Learning Analytics (BDLA) deals with the use and implementation of big data techniques and tools in learning design and exploration in learning context. Today, in the time of creation of large numbers of digital learning platforms, virtual classrooms and online tests and assessments, educational institutions continually produce large amount of data. Such records consist of records of student performance, notes of their interactions, course relevant information and records of feedback loops. BDLA uses these datasets to get best predictions of students’ behavior, activity and their performance. The greatest strength in BDLA therefore, is in its noble capacity and capability to extract value from big, varied, complex data within velocity so as to deliver executive decisions that are otherwise beyond conventional analytical techniques. The fields of education where BDLA has been employed are presented in the figure below in an overview. For example, in the case of the personalized approach, the BDLA algorithms study the effectiveness and preferences of the individual learner to present subject content suitable for him or her. Still, similar technologies are applied in the systems like Khan Academy and Coursera to suggest the next course or the next exercise to complete. Furthermore, one of the BDLA solutions, namely, predictive analytics is designed to predict such outcomes as student dropout rate. Analyzing the history of such groups of students, predictive analysis allows identifying the students which would be in danger of leaving and, therefore, change the situation so that they stay in college. Another important use is in staff development; there is absolutely no doubt that improved curriculum is another important area of use. Through the evaluation of feedback information and the performance indicators, the structures of the course may be improved. In addition, other administrative issues like resource management and development, budgeting and financial control among others are managed by BDLA to give an institution functionalities to run effectively as it emphasizes on efficient student outcomes.
